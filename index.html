Welcome!

Hey there, I'm Karina. I am a Software Developer and a contemporary artist living in NYC. As a programmer, I focus on creating Java programs for clients. I have worked with frameworks such as Spring and Hibernate to create microservices and with languages such as Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and SQL. I am also learning Swift in order to create iOS applications and make my app ideas into a reality soon. You can find out more in my GitHub and my resume.

I have always been a curious individual, interested in various subjects with a desire to positively contribute to the community. I became interested in programming when I learned HTML and CSS at 14 in order to create a website where I could connect with other teens around the country. I went to LaGuardia Arts High School as an art major student and learned how to express myself as an artist using different forms of media. I believe that learning how to create my ideas as an artist helped me realize that I want to produce something that could help others. As a developer, I will create iOS applications to accomplish this.

Besides coding, I am a passionate painter and aspiring cartoonist. I enjoy painting portraits and landscapes and I am currently working on creating iOS stickers. I enjoy the outdoors and traveling. There's so much on this planet and so little time therefore every day is precious! I owe a big part of my self-growth to music so you might find some music rants and recommendations on here as well.
